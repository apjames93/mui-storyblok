##### MuiCardActions has the ability to render custom components.

MuiCardActions Examples:

```js
const content=[{
  button: [{
    buttonText: 'Mui Card Action Button',
    component: 'MuiButton',
  }],
  component: 'MuiButtonDialog',
  dialog: [{
    component: 'MuiDialog',
    dialogTitle: [{
      component: 'MuiDialogTitle',
      text: 'Dialog Title',
    }],
  }],
}];
```
